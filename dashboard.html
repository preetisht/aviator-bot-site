How to Use Your Key

  1. Install the Chrome extension
  2. Click the extension icon on any Aviator game page
  3. Enter your license key when prompted
  4. Your tier and features will activate immediately

Key validates every 24 hours. Works on up to 2-3 devices (depending on plan).

FAQ

Does this guarantee profit?

No. The game has a 3% house edge. No bot can change that. This bot maximizes your chance of hitting a short-term target and stopping. It's discipline automation, not a money printer.

Can I share my license key?

Each key is tied to a limited number of devices (1-3 depending on plan). If you exceed the limit, validation will fail. You can deactivate old devices from the dashboard.

What if I lose internet during a session?

The bot has a 48-hour grace period. If you validated within the last 48 hours, it will continue working offline. After that, it locks until reconnection.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel through Razorpay/Stripe and your access continues until the current billing period ends.

Is my data safe?

We store only your email and device fingerprint (a hash). No passwords, no game data, no bankroll information touches our servers.
